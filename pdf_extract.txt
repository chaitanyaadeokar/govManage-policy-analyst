Traditional governance systems rely heavily on manual processes, which often leads
to slow decision-making, lack of transparency, and difficulty in monitoring policy
compliance. Coordination between departments is also challenging, causing delays
in approvals and workflow management. Due to the absence of automated systems
for risk assessment and policy validation, governance processes become inefficient
and difficult to track. Therefore, there is a need for an Agentic AI-based
Governance Management System that can automate governance workflows,
improve transparency, and enhance accountability in organizational decision-

MethodologyPolicy Analyst Agent:
Purpose:
The Policy Analyst Agent processes governance policies and extracts key information to
evaluate policy effectiveness and identify areas that require review.
Functions:
Parses policy documents using NLP.
Extracts key policy rules, objectives, and constraints.
Performs semantic analysis using embedding models to identify policy relevance.
Detects outdated or conflicting policies within the governance framework.
Output:
Extracted policy insights
List of policies requiring revision
Structured policy analysis report
--- Page 11 ---
MethodologyRisk Assessment Agent:
Purpose:
Evaluate potential governance risks related to policies and decisions.
Functions:
Receives policy insights from the Policy Analyst Agent.
Uses predefined governance risk parameters.
Calculates a risk score based on policy impact, compliance gaps, and operational risk
factors.
Identifies weak governance controls.
Classifies risks into Low, Medium, or High categories.
Stores calculated risk data in the system database.
Output:
Risk score
Risk classification report
List of weak governance controls
--- Page 12 ---
MethodologyCompliance Agent:
Purpose:
 The Compliance Agent verifies whether governance processes comply with regulatory
requirements and internal policies.
 Functions :
Matches governance actions with predefined compliance rules and standards.
Detects violations, missing approvals, and policy inconsistencies.
Identifies pending audits or compliance gaps.
Generates compliance status reports.
Output:
Compliance status
List of violations or pending actions
--- Page 13 ---
MethodologyAudit Agent:
Purpose:
 The Audit Agent ensures transparency by maintaining records of governance activities and
system decisions.  
 Functions:
Logs governance decisions and workflow activities.
Maintains audit trails for policy analysis, risk evaluation, and compliance checks.
Stores audit data in the governance database.
Enables traceability and accountability for governance decisions.
Output:
Audit logs
Decision trace records
--- Page 14 ---
MethodologyFeedback & Improvement Agent:
Purpose:
 This agent continuously improves the governance system by analyzing feedback and system
performance.
 Functions:
Monitors performance of all agents.
Collects feedback from governance outputs and reports.
Identifies inefficiencies in the workflow.
Updates agent parameters and optimization strategies.
Output:
System performance insights
Recommendations for improving governance processes
